Unit 1 Vocabulary

Addiction

IPA: /əˈdɪkʃn/

Part of Speech: Noun (U or C)

Definition: the condition of being unable to stop using or doing something as a habit, especially something harmful

Example: He is now fighting his addiction to alcohol.

Advertise

IPA: /ˈædvətaɪz/

Part of Speech: Verb (I or T)

Definition: to tell the public about a product or a service in order to encourage people to buy or to use it

Example: If you want to attract customers you need to advertise.
